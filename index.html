FAQ

Everything you need to know

Do I need to travel to Hong Kong?
+
No, the entire process is handled remotely. You do not need to travel to Hong Kong. Everything is managed online through our secure platform.
What documents are required?
+
Three documents: a copy of your valid passport, a selfie holding your open passport, and recent proof of address (under 3 months). All uploaded via your dashboard.
How long does formation take?
+
48 to 72 business hours after receiving your complete documents. Track progress in real time on your dashboard.
Is bank account opening guaranteed?
+
We work with partner banks and optimise your file to maximise approval chances. In case of refusal, we offer alternatives.
Can I run an international business?
+
Yes. A Hong Kong company is ideal for trade, consulting, e-commerce, or asset management — favourable tax environment and strong global banking reputation.
How does the affiliate programme work?
+
You receive a unique link. Every client through your link earns you a commission. Monthly payments by wire transfer or crypto.
